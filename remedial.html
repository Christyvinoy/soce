Remedial Coaching


As per UGC guidelines of XIth plan, remedial coaching programme was started in CUSAT in the year 2008. This programme was implemented for the benefit of SC/ST /OBC (Non â€“creamy layer) minorities and economically weaker section of students of CUSAT. To monitor the programme an advisory committee was formed with Vice Chancellor as chairman and five professors as members.



Objective

To improve the academic proficiency of the students in various subjects and to raise their level of comprehension in such subjects where quantitative, Technical and laboratory works are involved.
